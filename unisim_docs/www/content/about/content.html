About us

The "UNISIM Virtual Platforms" research project is currently maintained by CEA List (Software Reliability Laboratory).

Genealogy

The "UNISIM Virtual Platforms" project has started several years ago within the UNISIM simulation environment. UNISIM provides academia and industry with a simulation platform to ease hardware/software codesign. UNISIM is particularly geared toward architectural exploration but we wanted to further focus our research on the hardware/software integration and validation. The "UNISIM Virtual Platforms" project is the visible results of these works.
