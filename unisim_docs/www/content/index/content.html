Overview

The "UNISIM Virtual Platforms" is a research cross-platform open source simulation platform. The applications of such platforms go from non-intrusive debugging of software, through hardware/software integration, to massive testing of software, all on a virtual hardware.

The UNISIM Virtual Platforms are based on industry standards IEEE1666TM, OSCI SystemCTM and OSCI SystemCTM TLM 2.0

Several open source simulators for different targets (PowerPC, ARM, Star12X, TMS320C3X) and different hosts (Linux, Windows, Mac OS X) are available for download.
